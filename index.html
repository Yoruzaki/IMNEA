About the Seminar

National Seminar on Integrated Management of Natural Ecosystems in Algeria: Sustainability, Resilience, and Restoration (IMNEA 2026)

📅 21–22 April 2026

📍 Grand Lecture Hall – Higher National School of Forests, Khenchela

📌 Organized by:

  • Higher National School of Forests – Khenchela
  • Laboratory Forest Algerian & Climate Change (LAFACC)
  • In partnership with CRSTRA – Biskra

Objectives

🌐

Interdisciplinary Dialogue

Create an interdisciplinary scientific forum on sustainable natural resource management

🔗

Integrated Approaches

Strengthen integrated approaches linking forests–soils–water–biodiversity

💻

Technological Innovation

Promote technological innovation (GIS, AI, Remote Sensing, Environmental Modeling)

Seminar Thematic Themes

🌲

Theme 1: Resilience and Sustainable Management of Forest Ecosystems

  • Climate change and forest ecosystem resilience
  • Post-fire restoration and wildfire prevention
  • Climate vulnerability and forest dieback
  • Economic valorization: timber, NTFPs, and the bioeconomy
  • Innovative silvicultural approaches and participatory management
🌍

Theme 2: Soils, Erosion, Desertification, and Water Security

  • The role of forests in water regulation and soil fertility
  • Land degradation, desertification, and salinization
  • Watershed management and hydro-forestry planning
  • Water stress, drought, and groundwater dynamics
  • Groundwater and integrated water resources management (IWRM)
🐾

Theme 3: Biodiversity, Conservation, and Habitat Restoration

  • Inventories, ecological monitoring, and threatened species
  • Fragmentation, overgrazing, and anthropogenic pressures
  • Ecological networks, habitat connectivity, and natural restoration
  • Pollution and ecological disturbances
💻

Theme 4: Scientific Innovation, GIS, and Digital Technologies

  • GIS, remote sensing, drones, LiDAR, and environmental AI
  • Big data and predictive modelling: wildfires, drought
  • Decision-support systems and ecosystem monitoring
  • Smart forestry and digital platforms
